> ZeroGPU's IAB taxonomy classifier is purpose-built for real-time content categorization at the edge. It maps text to the IAB Content Taxonomy, the industry standard used across programmatic advertising enabling instant, low-latency content classification without roundtrips to a centralized server. At 90M parameters on ONNX, it's optimized for the kind of high-volume, sub-millisecond classification that ad tech and content platforms demand. Deploy it on edge devices to classify content in-stream, at the point of interaction.
