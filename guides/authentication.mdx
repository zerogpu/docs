---
title: "Authentication"
description: "How to authenticate with ZeroGPU APIs."
---

All ZeroGPU requests require two headers:

- `x-api-key`: your API key
- `x-project-id`: your project ID

You can create or rotate both in the ZeroGPU dashboard once your waitlist access is approved.

## Best practices

- Keep keys secret and store them in server-side environment variables.
- Avoid shipping keys in client-side apps unless your use case explicitly allows it.
- Rotate keys immediately if you believe they were exposed.
