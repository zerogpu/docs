These guides describe how to connect ZeroGPU to popular platforms. Official, first-class integrations may be added later.

## Common configuration

- Base URL: `https://api.zerogpu.ai`
- Headers: `x-api-key` and `x-project-id`
- Model name: choose from the [Model Catalog](/models/overview)
